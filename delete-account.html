Account Deletion – Account Book App

Account Book respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data. Users can permanently delete their account and all associated data directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Account Book app
  2. Go to User Profile from bottom tabs
  3. Select Delete User Account
  4. Confirm the deletion
Note: Account deletion is immediate and irreversible.

What Data Is Deleted

Once you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • User authentication data (Firebase Authentication)
  • All financial records such as income, expenses.
  • Any other data associated with your account in our database

Data Retention

Account Book does not retain any personal or financial data after account deletion.
